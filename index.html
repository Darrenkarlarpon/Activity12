Bachelor in Computer Technology

The BACT is a four-year degree program that focuses on the practical aspects of the science of computing. The Program develops core skills in multimedia technology, web mobile development and networks system administration. Furthermore, it prepares learners with supervisory and management skills. The curriculum is derived from BS Industrial Technology major in Computer Technology with CHED Certificate of Program Compliance (COPC) No. 31, series of 2021.

Program Objectives:
The Program prepare learners with the
practical skills in multimedia technology, web/
mobile development, and network/systems
administration, and supervisorial skills to
manage an organization in these areas of
competencies, engage in a startup business
or pursue a teacher career.
Specific objectives:
The learner should be able to:
  1. Demostrate understanding of the importance of being an ethical computing
    professional when making work-related decisions.
  2. Develop products or processes by applying their knowlegde in computer or network
    systems, and development of productivity tools.
  3. Participate as an active member of a development/service team with leardership roles
    when apropriate.
  4. Apply communication skills effectively to promote ideas, goals, processes or products.
  5. Make positive contributions to the community with the skilld and abilities learned in
    the Program.
  6. Pursue lifelong learning for personal and professional growth through varied
    experiential and field-based oppurtunities.
